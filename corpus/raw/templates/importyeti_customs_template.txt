=== C. FIELD-SET SUMMARY (for building a synthetic-from-real-template row) ===
date | consignee (name+address, often truncated/garbled) | shipper/supplier (name+address+country)
| notify party (name+address, sometimes a customs broker, not the real end-user) | shipping port
(origin, +internal code) | port of entry (destination, +internal code) | place of receipt
(can mismatch the shipping port in linked metadata) | HS code (+ site's own HS description,
sometimes a second HS code appears only in the raw free-text and gets silently dropped from the
canonical field) | product description -- BOTH a cleaned/dedup'd version AND a raw OCR-messy
version with embedded broker fax/email/PO numbers/SAP codes/invoice numbers/customs seal numbers
| quantity + unit (e.g. "95 CTN") | weight (kg, unit sometimes unlabeled) | carrier (name + SCAC
code) | vessel name + IMO + flag country | voyage number | container id(s) + type + load status
+ service type | B/L number | estimated arrival date (YYYYMMDD, no separators) | shipping
cost/rate (route, ticker, booking date, value) | CIF (sometimes "0"/blank) | US clearing district
(can be geographically inconsistent with the actual port). Real messiness to imitate: broken word
wrap ("Denve R Co"), inconsistent capitalization mid-word ("Jac Ket", "Ontact"), duplicate/second
HS codes buried only in free text, notify-party contact info bleeding into the cargo-description
field, internal metadata cross-links pointing to the wrong city/country, and a "clearing district"
field that doesn't match the actual discharge port.
