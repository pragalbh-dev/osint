Woody Island

Woody Island is a rock/island located in the Paracel Islands. China first took possession of the feature in 1955.
U.S. Board of Geographic Names: Woody Island
China: Yongxing Dao, 永兴岛
Taiwan: Yongxing Island, 永興島
Vietnam: Đảo Phú Lâm
Occupied by: China
Legal Status: Rock/Island
GPS: 16° 50' 4" N, 112° 20' 23" E
