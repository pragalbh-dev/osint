RECONSTRUCTED TEXT — ISPR PRESS RELEASE, 14 OCTOBER 2021
================================================================================

[PARAPHRASE — lede/dateline, per S1]
KARACHI: General Qamar Javed Bajwa, Chief of the Army Staff (COAS), visited
the Army Air Defence Centre Karachi on Thursday, 14 October 2021. On arrival,
the COAS laid a floral wreath at the Shuhada monument.

[PARAPHRASE — per S1, S3]
He later witnessed the commissioning of the state-of-the-art, Chinese-origin
HQ-9/P HIMADS (High to Medium Air Defence System) into Pakistan Army Air
Defence. Lieutenant General Hamood-uz-Zaman Khan, Commander Army Air Defence
Command, briefed the COAS on the strategic weapon system.

[VERBATIM - HIGH CONFIDENCE — quoted by Army Recognition (S3) as "the
official communiqué read"; corroborated in substance by S1, S4, S6]
"Induction of HIMADS will significantly enhance Comprehensive Layered
Integrated Air Defence (CLIAD) shield of aerial frontiers of Pakistan as the
system is fully integrated through a well-knit Digitised System on its
inventory."

[VERBATIM - HIGH CONFIDENCE — matching wording and clause order across S5 and
an independent search-derived rendering, including the distinctive Pakistani-
English plural "aircrafts"]
"Capable of intercepting multiple air targets including aircrafts, cruise
missiles and Beyond Visual Range Weapons at ranges over 100 kilometers with
Single Shot Kill Probability, HQ-9/P is considered as a strategic long range
Surface to Air Missile (SAM) with remarkable flexibility and precision."

[PARAPHRASE — COAS remarks on air defence, per S1, S4]
The COAS said that induction of high-tech systems will make Pakistan's air
defence
[VERBATIM word/phrase — quoted identically in S1 and S4]
"impenetrable in the emerging threat scenario."

[PARAPHRASE, with one quoted word — per S1]
He said exemplary synergy between Pakistan Air Force and Pakistan Army Air
Defence makes the country's air defence "impregnable."

[VERBATIM - HIGH CONFIDENCE — quoted identically in S2 and S4]
"The strategic partnership and defence collaboration between Pakistan and
China is a factor of stability in the region."

[PARAPHRASE — closing line, per S1]
Senior officials from China were also present on the occasion.
