Purpose: A short REAL example of official "routine exercise / routine
activity" euphemistic framing, for use as a contradiction template in the
Pakistan HQ-9/P OSINT corpus (i.e., official language that downplays/normalizes
a militarily or strategically significant act by labeling it "routine").
Access date for all sources below: 2026-07-16.

Two independent, real, dated examples are given: one Pakistani (ISPR), one
Chinese (PLA Eastern Theater Command). Both are contemporaneous (April 2026)
and both use "routine" as the operative framing word for an act that
outside commentary treated as strategically notable.

Example 1: significant/reactive military activity described as
administratively unremarkable.
